Recruitment Services

Recruitment Services is an off the shelf product where we understand client requirements and provide them with candidates that fit their vacancy at a pre-negotiated price. The hirer merely needs to interview these candidates and make their choice as we do the pre-screening based on the job description and parameters shared with us. The usual pricing mechanism here is a fee, which is a percentage of the salary of candidates hired through the use of this service.
