Staffing Services

Staffing Services ends the administrative and legal burden of managing employees on your payroll. You may have a preselected group of employees or plan to recruit employees in the field of sales, customer sevice, accounts, administration staff etc that you choose not to have on your payrolls, Duarz Staffing Servcies provides the ideal opportunity for reduced manpower and administrative costs.

Staffing Services is also designed keeping in mind that business work in an environment, which can cause spikes, thereby creating a need for hands to handle the temporary increase in the workload. Whatever, the reason for such temporary hires we have built databases of people who can come in for that required period.

Our Staffing services is compliant with all statutory requirements covering PF, ESIC, PT, Labour Welfare, CLRA, etc across various states of India.
